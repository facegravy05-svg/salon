Our Philosophy

More than a salon —
a private retreat

Do Salon & Spa is a boutique beauty sanctuary nestled in Sandy Springs, Georgia, built on the belief that every client deserves an extraordinary experience. Our intimate, sparkling-clean suite is a place where you are seen, heard, and celebrated — where the atmosphere is as relaxing as the results are remarkable.

Our stylists — Marina, Jade, and founder Amanda Lee — each bring a rare depth of expertise and a genuine passion for their craft. From Marina's internationally influenced color techniques to Jade's decade-long loyal following to Amanda's mastery of fine and curly hair, every appointment is in gifted hands.

Amanda Lee
Owner & Founder · Do Salon & Spa
Our Services

Curated for the refined woman

Each service is a bespoke experience, tailored to the unique character and needs of your hair and skin. We use only the most prestigious professional products available.

01 ✂️

Precision Cut & Style

A thorough consultation followed by an architecture-level cut that flatters your bone structure. Finished with a bespoke blowout.

02 🎨

Luxury Color & Highlights

Parisian balayage, dimensional foiling, and expert toning to achieve luminous, living color that transcends the ordinary.

Keratin & Smoothing

Restore silkiness and manageability with a bespoke keratin treatment. Six weeks of effortless, frizz-free hair with exceptional shine.

04 💨

Blowouts

05 💐

Bridal & Occasion
