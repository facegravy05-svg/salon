Our Philosophy

More than a salon —
a private retreat

Salon Doo was founded on a singular belief: that the women who walk through our doors deserve nothing less than extraordinary. With over two decades of serving a distinguished clientele, we have refined the art of personal beauty into something deeply personal — a ritual of self-care that honors your time, your individuality, and your grace.

Our master stylists are among the most sought-after in the region, trained in the finest international techniques and dedicated to the meticulous craft of making you look and feel your absolute best.

Éloise Deveraux
Founder & Creative Director, Salon Doo
